Quick Actions

How To Use

1. Tap Add Product to create a new rice item with category, capital, and profit.
2. Tap Add New Stocks to record delivered stock by category without opening the full daily report.
3. Tap Edit Beginning Rice to enter the starting sacks or kilos for today.
4. Tap Update Prices when you want to change the selling price quickly.
5. Tap Delete Product only if you want to permanently remove a rice item.
